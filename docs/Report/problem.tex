This chapter aims to explain the context of the project, that is, to demonstrate how the logging of ascents are made, how the boulders are managed, and how gym managers can map their boulders.


\section{Ascents}

The main record kept by coaches is the record of training sessions. This record is done
manually, meaning the coach has to write in a notebook or on an Excel sheet all the training
sessions completed by the athletes. This record is made for each individual athlete and for
each training session performed. The coach must take into account numerous parameters,
such as the date of the training session, the type of training (for example, whether it was
a surf workout or a gym workout), and the exercises performed or the surfer activity (PE:
maneuvers executed).
